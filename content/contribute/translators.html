Translators

Currently, only the Android port of EasyRPG Player has proper translation support. However, there is no central place to submit translations to. This will hopefully be fixed in the future.

In the meanwhile, you can download and translate the raw strings file in your native language and submit it to us.
We currently have maintainers for French, German and Spanish language.

There are no additional texts ready and pending to be translated, but there will be later, for example for this information website or for EasyRPG Editor. Please come back later.
